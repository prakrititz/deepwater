Deepwater bringing purity through technology

Deepwater is an innovative open-source vision model designed to detect microplastics in aquatic environments. Leveraging advanced machine learning techniques, Deepwater empowers researchers, environmentalists, and citizen scientists to identify and quantify microscopic plastic particles in water samples. This powerful tool is freely accessible to anyone, promoting collaborative efforts in addressing the global issue of microplastic pollution. With its user-friendly interface and robust detection capabilities, Deepwater aims to accelerate research, raise awareness, and contribute to the development of effective solutions for mitigating the impact of microplastics on our ecosystems.
